BMI Classification

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a numerical value of a person's weight in relation to their height. It is a widely used screening tool to categorize individuals into different weight status categories.

Underweight: BMI less than 18.5

Normal Weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9

Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9

Obese: BMI of 30 or greater
